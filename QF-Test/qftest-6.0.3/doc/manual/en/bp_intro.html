Introduction

This part of the manual describes best practices based on lessons learned from several customer projects and user feedback. The concepts described should assist you in finding the best strategy for using QF-Test in your projects.

Note QF-Test is a very generic tool. The hints and experiences described here are just suggestions from our point of view, which we hope will support you in working efficiently and successfully with QF-Test in your project. But they are just one way of doing things and you will have to find your own solution that works best for your specific project.
